The tour will take approximately 75-minutes, with visitors encouraged to arrive 15 minutes prior to their tour starting.

All tours will start from inside Stadium Square (Level 1, Gate 2/3).

How often does the Tour run?

Tours operate Monday to Friday, outside major event days, with three daily sessions available at 10am, 12pm, and 2pm. click here to book.

Is the Tour suitable for children and families?

Yes, the tour is suitable for children and families of all ages, provided children aged under 15 years are accompanied by a paying adult. The tour route is also stroller-friendly and accessible for families with young children.

Is the Tour accessible?

Yes, the tour is accessible for guests with mobility needs. The tour route is wheelchair and stroller friendly, with lift access available throughout the experience. If you have any specific accessibility requirements, we recommend contacting the team ahead of your visit so they can best assist you.

View Marvel Stadium Accessibility Map (PDF)

Where does the Tour start?

Check-in begins inside Stadium Square at Level 1, Gate 2/3 of Marvel Stadium. Our reception team and tour guides will welcome and check you in at the desk upon arrival. Security staff will also be on-site, and the tour will depart directly from this location.

What is the cancellation or refund policy?

Guests who cancel more than 48-hours prior to their tour will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48-hours of the tour are not eligible for a refund, however a credit note may be issued at management discretion. No-shows are not eligible for a refund or credit. Guests arriving after the tour has departed may not be guaranteed entry and no refund will apply.

In the event of a venue-initiated cancellation, guests will be offered either a rescheduled session or a full refund. If access to certain tour areas is restricted due to operational requirements, refunds will not apply provided the tour can still reasonably operate.

Refunds or exchanges will not be provided for change of mind, inability to attend, late arrival, weather conditions, partial tour route changes, or changes to accessible areas or tour inclusions.
