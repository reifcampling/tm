FAQ’s

When do tickets general access and hospitality tickets go on sale?
Hospitality Public Sale: Tuesday 7 April at 10:00AM AEST, General Access Public Sale: Wednesday 8 April at 10:00AM AEST
Are there travel and accommodation packages?
Yes. Ticket + Hotel packages are available through Sportsnet Holidays, combining your game ticket with accommodation. Packages on sale Monday 6 of April at 10:00AM AEST. You can view Sportsnet options here sportsnetholidays.com. If you are a fan travelling from the USA, On Location are the official hospitality partner of the NFL and you can view experience offerings here onlocationexp.com/nfl/nfl-australia-tickets
What is the difference between General Access and Hospitality?
General Access is an allocated seat only. Standing room from $95pp, General Tickets from $140.

Hospitality includes a premium seat plus food and beverage at one of the MCG's iconic hospitality venues. Hospitality packages are from $1,750 per person
When and where is the game?
The NFL 2026 Melbourne Game will be on Friday 11 September 2026 for a 10.35AM kick-off. Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG)
Which teams are playing?
San Francisco 49ers v Los Angeles Rams.
Are accessible tickets available?
Tickets can be purchased through the Ticketmaster Website, or by submitting a request through the Ticketmaster Help Centre following these steps:

1. From the dropdown menu, select Accessible Bookings as the topic.
2. Then select Los Angeles Rams v San Francisco 49ers as the event name.

Alternatively, you can call our Accessible Booking Line on 1300 446 925, available Monday to Sunday, 9:00 am - 5:00 pm AEDT. Submitting a request doesn't guarantee a ticket, availability and type of accessible tickets will vary by venue. A Ticketmaster representative will be in touch if further information is required.

For more information, visit our help pageAccessible Tickets: Everything you need to know.
What are the hospitality packages and where can I purchase them?
All hospitality packages are sold through Ticketmaster. Each room offers a different game day experience. Review the full hospitality guide on this page: ticketmaster.com.au/NFL to find the package that best suits your group.
How many tickets can I purchase per transaction?
General Access: up to 6 tickets per transaction. Hospitality: up to 10 tickets per transaction.
When will I receive my tickets?
After completing your purchase, your tickets will be available to manage via your Ticketmaster account. Please note that ticket barcodes will not be displayed until 30 days prior to the game.
Who are the official sellers?
All tickets are sold exclusively through Ticketmaster ticketmaster.com.au/NFL. Ticket + Hotel packages are available through official partner Sportsnet Holidays sportsnetholidays.com. If you are a fan travelling from the USA, On Location are the official hospitality partner of the NFL and you can view experience offerings here onlocationexp.com/nfl/nfl-australia-tickets Only purchase through official channels.
Will there be fan events?
Fan events and activations surrounding the NFL Melbourne game will be announced at a later date.
