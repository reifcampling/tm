FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

EVENT INFORMATION

WHICH TEAMS ARE TAKING PART?
All HSBC SVNS events will showcase the best men's and women's rugby sevens teams, including Australia Argentina, Canada, Fiji, France, Great Britain, Japan, New Zealand, Spain, South Africa, and USA
WILL I BE ABLE TO LEAVE AND RE-ENTER THE STADIUM ON THE DAY?
Great news for all attendees of the Perth SVNS! We are pleased to announce that re-admittance will be permitted throughout the event. Whether you need to step out for a break or handle personal matters, rest assured that you can re-enter and continue enjoying the festivities without any hassle. We aim to make your experience as convenient and enjoyable as possible.

PRICES & INFORMATION

ARE THERE ANY AGE RESTRICTIONS FOR ATTENDING THE EVENT?
All children aged 4+ are required to have a valid ticket upon entry to the stadium. Children aged under 4 years are not required to have a ticket, given they will be seated on a parent's lap and not occupy a seat. The Party Stand is an 18+ Only event space.
WHAT IS THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF TICKETS THAT I CAN PURCHASE?
The maximum number of tickets you can purchase in a single transaction is 20.

ONLINE TICKET & DELIVERY

HOW AND WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY TICKETS?
Tickets will be issued digitally and we will communicate with you closer to the event date when your tickets are ready to be downloaded.
WILL I BE REQUIRED TO BRING ID FOR ENTRY?
Yes, ID may be required to confirm proof of age.

FAMILIES

IS THERE A FAMILY ZONE IN THE STADIUM AND WHERE IS IT LOCATED?
HSBC SVNS brings the party across the whole stadium. Do not book tickets in the Party Stand if you are attending with families as this space is 18+ Only.
