FAQs

  1. Sign in to My Account, click or tap My Tickets then select your event.
  2. Click the Sell button*.
  3. Select and price the ticket(s) you'd like to sell.
  4. Tell us how you'd like to get paid.
  5. Review your listing and you're all set.

*Some events may not allow their tickets to be resold, or certain ticket types may not be resold — this decision is made by the Event Organiser.

Not all events or ticket types are approved for resale by the Event Organiser — they have the discretion to change resale options at any time. VIP tickets and collector tickets can't be resold. If resale is not available, you'll see a message detailing that the Event Organiser has not enabled resale for your event, and therefore selling your tickets on Ticketmaster is not an option. To maintain the authenticity and security of tickets, fans cannot resell tickets that have been previously purchased through resale.

If you're removing a listing on Ticketmaster.com.au, follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to My Account.
  2. Tap My Tickets
  3. Tap your event to view your tickets
  4. Tap Cancel Listing
  5. Confirm and complete

If you're removing a listing using the Ticketmaster App, follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to My Account.
  2. Tap My Tickets
  3. Tap your event to view your tickets
  4. Tap Cancel Listing
  5. Confirm and complete

To edit a listing, you must cancel and re-list via the steps above.

To check on the status of your listed tickets, just sign in to My Account. To get paid for the tickets you sell make sure your account is up to date with a valid payment method. If you’ve received notification your tickets have sold. Hyperwallet, our payment provider will process payment to your chosen payment in 10-12 business days. Please allow an additional 3-5 business days for funds to process through to your nominated payment method.

Creating a listing to sell your tickets is always free. When your tickets are sold, a seller fee is collected from the total selling price of your tickets. During the listing process you'll be able to preview all listing details, including the seller fee.
