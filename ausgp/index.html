Joy Flight

Expect Nothing Less

Extra Experience

Want to enjoy the excitement and thrill of a helicopter flight whilst visiting the FORMULA 1 QATAR AIRWAYS AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX 2026?

PHS will be conducting our highly popular Joy Flights throughout this year's event. These sensational scenics provide a safe and spectacular way to absorb all the excitement from above!

The flight takes you on one of our most scenic flight patterns around the entire Albert Park Circuit. You'll have breathtaking views of the Melbourne CBD and Port Phillip Bay.

Whether it's your first time in a helicopter or you're already a seasoned flyer, the fun of a flight at this year's Grand Prix is not to be missed!

Tour Inclusions:

  • Two laps of the Grand Prix Circuit (approximately 6 - 8 minutes).
  • Joy Flights operate Thursday between 10am - 5pm and Friday - Sunday between 9am - 5pm, weather permitting.
  • Departures from our exclusive helipad situated next to Gate 10.


Terms & Conditions:

  • Joy Flights operate Thursday between 10am - 5pm and Friday - Sunday between 9am - 5pm, weather permitting.
  • Tickets are valid for one (1) Joy Flight per person and are valid strictly on the date you select at time of purchase
  • Present your Joy Flight ticket to the staff at the PHS Helipad to redeem.
  • Flight routes are constant, flight times are approximate only and may vary according to helicopter type, number of passengers and/or weather conditions.
  • Patrons under the age of 16 must be accompanied by an adult with a valid Joy Flight ticket.
  • Items larger than a handbag cannot be taken on the flight. Limited storage of larger bags is available at the helipad. PHS take no responsibility for patrons' items stored.
  • Due to safety protocols there is no photography allowed on the helipad before boarding. Once in the aircraft mobile phones and small cameras are permitted.
  • Tickets are redeemed on a first come first saved basis, no time allocations can be pre booked in advance.
  • Please allow at least 30 - 60 minutes for safety briefing and manifesting to the aircraft, depending on demand. We recommend your attend the PHS Helipad one hour before you intend to fly.
  • This flight ticket is not an event ticket. A ticket to the FORMULA 1 QATAR AIRWAYS AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX 2026 is required to gain access to our Joy Flights located within the grounds.
  • Tickets cannot be redeemed for cash, are non-refundable (Excluding weather events preventing flights) and not exchangeable.
  • For further terms and conditions visit www.phs.com.au.

Collector Tickets

Make your entrance to the FORMULA 1 QATAR AIRWAYS AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX 2026 truly unforgettable with a stylish Collector Ticket. Not only will these tickets grant you access to the event, but they also serve as a cherished keepsake to remember for years to come. Please note that Collector Tickets can only be purchased with an Australian Postal address, so make sure your Ticketmaster account details are up to date. Expected delivery - 4 weeks prior to the event.

Collector Tickets incur an additional $25 fee per ticket.

What are Aisle Seat Tickets?

Looking to add some added comfort to your race day? We've got you covered with Aisle Seat Tickets!

Aisle seats are the closest 4 seats to each aisle and cost an additional $25. This is also reflected in the seats at the back of an aisle which do not have any seats in front of them.
