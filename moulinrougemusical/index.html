FAQS

Who are the authorised ticket sellers for Moulin Rouge! The Musical
The authorised ticket sellers for Moulin Rouge! The Musical are Ticketmaster and Sydney Lyric Theatre. Click here to access tickets.

Customers are encouraged to avoid buying tickets through unauthorised third-party websites that sell secondary tickets, as there is no guarantee the tickets will be valid for entry at the venue. This includes unauthorised re-sellers such as Viagogo, Ticketbis, eBay, Gumtree, Tickets Australia, Queen of Tickets, Ticketblaster, Ticket Merchant or similar. In NSW it is illegal to resell a ticket for more than 110% of the face value of the ticket, as regulated by NSW Fair Trading.
How much do tickets cost?
Ticket prices for Moulin Rouge! The Musical vary by seating location, performance date and time.

Ticket prices start from $89, with no additional booking fees. Pay only the price you see on the ticket.

For the best value tickets, consider midweek performances.
Can I exchange my tickets?
We know plans can change, that’s why we’ve made it simple for you to exchange your tickets to a different date, subject to the below Terms and Conditions.

To exchange your tickets for Moulin Rouge! The Musical, please contact your ticketing agent at point of purchase.

Ticketmaster customers, please contact Ticketmaster via the help desk

Terms and Conditions:

Tickets may only be exchanged for performances within the same venue and season. Exchanges must be made no later than 7 days prior to the start time of your original ticketed performance.

Tickets may be exchanged for any other single performance on sale at the time of exchange, subject to seat availability, blackout dates, sold-out performances and other restrictions. Exchanges may not be made for the same performance.

Exchanges will only be made for tickets purchased through the authorised ticket seller for the venue. Tickets purchased via third-party providers are subject to their exchange policies, we recommend contacting them directly.

Exchanges will be valued at the original face value of the ticket purchased. Additional payments may apply if choosing a performance or price level that costs more than the original ticket value, or if original ticket value was from an originally discounted ticket into a performance with no discounts available.

New special offers may not be applied to exchanged tickets.

Exchanges may not be made to reduce the total number of tickets originally purchased.

Upon exchange, original tickets will be voided and will not allow access into the performance. Customers must present newly issued tickets for entry into the performance.

Complimentary tickets and resold tickets are not eligible for exchange.

Are Group bookings available?
Groups of all sizes are welcome at Moulin Rouge! The Musical. If your party is over 9 people, please enquire here.
Is there an age minimum?
The show is recommended for children 12 years of age and over.
What is the duration of the performance?
The show running time is 2 hours and 35 minutes including a 20-minute interval.
Where can I find accessibility information?
Wheelchair and attendant seating can be purchased online; wheelchair-suitable areas are indicated on the interactive map with the use of the wheelchair symbol. Patrons that use a companion card or have other accessible requirements can call 1300 795 267 or follow steps here.

For more information on planning your visit and Sydney Lyric Theatre’s service and amenities, as well as its policies, please visit the venue’s website.
How long is the show running in Sydney?

Does Moulin Rouge! The Musical contain special effects?
Yes. Please be advised the show contains flashing lights, strobe lights, fog, loud bangs and the appearance of firearms onstage.
