Big Bush BBQ

Event Extra

The beloved sausage sizzle takes on new meaning at the Big Bush BBQ, where every dollar goes back into rural communities. Each day of the Show, a different NSW Show Society steps up to fire up the grill and fundraise for their local community.

Visitors can enjoy a delicious sausage sizzle while knowing their contribution directly helps create positive change for our friends in the bush. It’s more than just a snack - it’s a way to give back!

Find us at the Schmidt Arena between 9am - 3:30pm.
